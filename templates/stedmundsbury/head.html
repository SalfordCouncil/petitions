The e-petitions facility in its current form will not be available after 6 December 2013.

From 6 December 2013, the Council will accept electronic petitions created using free software that is available online. A number of websites provide this service which can be easily found by using an internet search engine. Completed petitions can be submitted to: democratic.services@stedsbc.gov.uk
